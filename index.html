Friendship day comes early, as Shah Rukh Khan gifts luxury car to Bhaijaan Salman Khan

Shah Rukh Khan and Salman Khan seem to give us major friendship goals. Friends turned foes are now BFFs in b-town. As their fans wait for the duo in a full fledged film, for now the two are happy doing special appearances in each other's film. After SRK's act of a magician in Tubelight, Salman will be seen in a special song for SRK's dwarf film. The two shot for it in Mumbai recently. And as per reports in BollywoodLife, Shah Rukh Khan surprised his superstar friend by gifting him a luxurious car for doing the favour.

Source: DNA India

Shah Rukh Khan reveals why he never went for club hopping before 'Jab Harry Met Sejal'!

Shah Rukh Khan, known for his workaholic nature, is not much of a party person. But why is it that he never hopped the clubs much? "I haven't been to many clubs. When I was in Delhi, I did not have enough money to do it and in Mumbai, I became a star. So here too, I could not do much of club hopping. It's Imtiaz, who has made me do these club hoppings, in Europe," he said, during the launch of the track Beech Beech Mein from the upcoming movie Jab Harry Met Sejal, at a night club on Monday night.
